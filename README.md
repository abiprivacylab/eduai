# EduAI — Privacy‑First, Multi‑Model AI for Students and Schools

EduAI is a privacy‑first, multi‑model AI learning platform designed to bring safe, age‑appropriate artificial intelligence to classrooms around the world. Built with a modern Next.js frontend and a modular AI backend, EduAI enables students to learn with guided explanations, structured assignments, and subject‑aware reasoning — all while giving schools full control over safety, privacy, and usage.

## 🌟 Mission
To empower students with responsible AI tools that enhance learning, support teachers, and maintain the highest standards of privacy and safety in educational environments.

## 🔒 Privacy‑First Design
EduAI is built on a strict privacy philosophy:
- No personal data is stored or used for training.
- All interactions pass through a dedicated safety layer.
- Age‑appropriate filters ensure safe, classroom‑ready responses.
- Schools can manage usage through a transparent credit system.

## 🧠 Multi‑Model Intelligence
EduAI intelligently routes tasks to the most suitable AI model:
- Lightweight models for simple explanations  
- Reasoning models for math, science, and logic  
- Advanced models for research, writing, and coding  
- Local or cloud‑based models depending on school requirements  

This ensures high‑quality responses while keeping costs predictable for large student groups.

## 🎓 Built for Real Classrooms
EduAI supports:
- Homework help and concept explanations  
- Reading comprehension and writing assistance  
- Math and science reasoning  
- Teacher‑generated assignments and quizzes  
- Safe chat for younger learners  
- School‑wide usage controls  

## 🏗️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js, TypeScript, TailwindCSS  
- **Backend (coming soon):** FastAPI, Python, model routing engine  
- **Safety:** Rule‑based filters + model‑based moderation  
- **Infrastructure:** Modular, scalable, privacy‑first
